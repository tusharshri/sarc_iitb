Co-founders, Chairmans and CEOs



Victor Menezes



B.Tech, Electrical Engineering, IIT Bombay in 1970
Master of Science degree in finance and economics from the Sloan School of the Massachusetts Institute of Technology in 1972
Senior Advisor with New Silk Route Partners LLC
Formerly Senior Vice Chairman of Citigroup Inc., and after a 32-year global career in the company, retired in January 2005
Donated $3 million to IIT Bombay Heritage Fund, in 2007, for building a world class convention centre (VMCC)

Ajit Ranade

Bachelors of Technology in department of electrical engineering from IIT Mumbai
MBA from the Indian Institute of Management, Ahmedabad, and a Ph.D in Economics from Brown University, USA
Chief Economist of the Aditya Birla Group, a diversified multinational conglomerate
He is a Director on the board of Hindalco Almex Aerospace Limited. He is also the Government of India's nominee Director on the Board of Multi Commodity Exchange of India Ltd. (MCX)
He was Chief Economist and Head of Microfinance Business at ABN AMRO Bank, India
He is an active member of industry chambers. He chairs the Research Advisory Committee of the Indian Institute of Banking and Finance
He is also a member of the Senate and the Advisory Council of IIT Bombay

Bharat Desai

Bachelors of Technology in department of electrical engineering from IIT Mumbai in 1975
Co-founder of Syntel, 1980
MBA in Finance from University of Michigan
The 20 crore target for Indoor Gymkhana Project was achieved throught magor contribution of Bharat Desai(7.5 crore)
Recognized as "Entrepreneur of the Year" by USA Today and NASDAQ
Ranked 239th in Frobes "richest people in US"
