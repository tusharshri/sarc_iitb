Jairam Ramesh



B.Tech in Mechanical Engineering (1975)
He studied public management at Carnegie Mellon University during 1975-77, at the Massachusetts Institute of Technology (MIT)
He is a founding member of the Indian School of Business in Hyderabad and is a member of the International Council of the New York-based Asia Society
He is a Member of Parliament representing Andhra Pradesh state in the Rajya Sabha since June, 2004
In July, 2011, Mr. Ramesh was elevated to the Union Council of Ministers of India and appointed Minister of Rural Development and Minister (Additional Charge) of the new Ministry of Drinking Water and Sanitation

Manohar Parrikar

B.tech in Metallurgical Engineering (1978)
Currently the chief minister of Goa
He is also credited with single handedly bringing the IFFI(International Film Festival of India) to Goa
He received "Distinguished Alumnus Award" by IIT Bombay in 2001 and was awarded “CNN-IBN Indian of the year 2012 “
He is named as the "Singham of Goa" after his speech at an Award ceremony when he addressed people “Dont worry, i will clean the political mess in the state”
