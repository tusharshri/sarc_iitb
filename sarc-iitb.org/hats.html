Hostel Alumni Team Stewardship


HATS is an important alumni-driven initiative that aims to channel the affinity and affection that many alumni have for their Hostels. HATS started with the Hostel 7 campaign "In Service of the Lady" in 2006 and continued with the Hostel 4 HATS campaign which was launched in 2009. Under the leadership of an enthusiastic "core team", campaigns have been successfully developed and implemented by all old hostels.


The objectives of HATS are:

  • Improve hostel infrastructure and facilities
  • Assist mess workers
  • Increase interaction between alumni and students
  • Organizing Reunions

HATS has become a critical part of hostel plannings.
Every hostel council is now looking towards this magical HAT of their Alumni
for their wishes that they couldn’t aquire by hostel funds.


      Achievements

    • Total funds collected till financial year 2012-13: Rs. 1.83 Crores
    • Total No of donors worldwide: 591
    • Hostels leading the race: 7, 4 and 8 (collectively 74%)
    • Mess workers helped by HATS: approx 250
    • Laundromats installed and inaugurated in H6 and H8
    • Basketball court built and inaugurated in H6
    • State of the art Gym purchased by H4
    • Bookshelves, designed by Rupee symbol designer Uday Kumar, installed in all rooms of H7
    • Digital camera, musical instruments, Home Theatre and Projector purchased by H2
    • Digital camera purchased by H3
    • Laundromats installed and inaugurated in H8
