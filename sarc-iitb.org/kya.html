Nandan Nilekani



B.Tech, Electrical Engineering, IIT Bombay in 1978
Co-founded Infosys Limited in 1981
Currently the chairman of the Unique Identification Authority of India (UIDAI)
Serves as Co-chairman of the IIT Bombay Heritage Fund
Listed among the world's 100 most influential people by Time magazine
Given the Padhma Bhushan, which is one of the most prestigious honors in India given to a civilian

Kanwal Rekhi

Graducated as an Electrical Engineer in 1967 from IIT Bombay
Co-founded and served as CEO of Excelan
Currently a board member of Pan IIT USA,Inc
Donated $3 million to IIT Bombay to set up a new School of Information Technology
Recipient of a Doctorate degree in Business and Engineering
Founding limited partner of infinity Capital India - a successful venture fund

Ashish Chauhan

Graduated from IIT Bombay with a bachelor's degree in Mechanical Engineering in 1989
Current Managing Director & Chief Executive Officer of the Bombay Stock Exchange (BSE)
Served as the CEO of Mumbai Indians, a cricket team in the Indian Premier League (IPL), as the CEO of Reliance Infosolutions
Donated $3 million to IIT Bombay to set up a new School of Information Technology
Serves on the Boards of directors of Central Depository Services Limited (CDSL), Bank of India Shareholding Limited (BOISL), BSE Institute Ltd(BIL) and Marketplace Technologies
