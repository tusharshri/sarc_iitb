Anupa Mittal

M.Sc. in Physics (gold medalist)
M.Phil in Physics (topper)
Bachalor of education
Computer qualified

Anupa Mittal have lived life freely having great and wonderful experiences in her live. She has a great husband and two loving children. She has never taken life for granted. From her every childhood she had a keen interest in studying, not all she loved studying she also loved helping other people with their studies. She made her parents proud by getting Gold Medal in M.Sc (Physics). After getting married to an Army officer she very generously involved studies in Army children's life along with its adventure. Travelling along with her husband to different places whether it be remote areas or metropolitan cities she has always helped children by teaching and by taking care of their welfare. She has long lasting rock solid teaching experience of all classes across different schools all over India.

While helping others she has always been a wonderful wife and kind hearted mother. She helped her sons in studies so that they perform outstandingly in their schooling and she helped them to get selected in top colleges of India (IIT Kanpur and IIT Bombay). She couldn't have asked for more.

This journey haven't been easy, it has it's challenges and difficulties hence she realized many parents share the same emotions. To help them and to make their children's life successful she started giving counselling and coaching to parents to how to motivate their children. She has also started preparing study materials for children, these material contains brain storming questions, challenge quizzes, puzzles etc from her previous students will helped them to clear their concepts and foundation for any competitive exam like IIT JEE

Strong Personality with stubborn Outlook

She has taken a lot of challenges in her life and have been successful in fulfilling her dreams. She believes every child is a genius he/she just need to be motivated and shown the right path and parents are closet to a child and can help them overcome his/her problems.
