Student Alumni Meet (SAM)

This event was conceptualized with a motive to keep in touch with IITB alumni. Here, students of IITB engage in direct telephonic conversations with our alumni. Each Phonathon is spread over few days (and nights too) and is conducted with a predefined goal at core and lots of lively and fun-filled conversations surrounding it.
General goals of Phonathons consist of publicizing various alumni events, fpdating the alumni database, fundraising, student mentorship etc. So far, in six years, a total of 22 Phonathons have been conducted reconnecting more than 19,000 Alumni around the globe. Stay tuned for the next Phonathon.

Reunions

The reunions for the Decennial and Silver Jubilee batches are held in the last week of December. Alumni from all over the globe converge at IITB to celebrate their reunion. The reunions include several formal and informal events spread over 2 - 3 days. Over the years, it has become a tradition for the Silver Jubilee batches to announce the legacy project of their batch during the reunions. The funds raised by the batch are utilized to support the initiatives chosen by the batch. The last Sunday of every year is reserved at IIT Bombay for welcoming former students of our institute. The celebration is known as Alumni Day.

Student Alumni Meet(SAM)

Student Alumni Meet (SAM) scheduled in the first weekend of October is the flagship event of SARC. Alumni from different fields are invited to the campus to interact with the student community. The sub-events of SAM are designed to cater to the needs of students and answer their queries about career options, placements, pursuing interests, networking, etc. Students are invited to participate in the events and discussions and make the best use of this opportunity to learn and experience the perspective of a person who has already been through the phase they are currently going through.
