Samwad - Trying to bring the conscience of the society inside the walls of the campus

Project Leader: Vishnu Vardhan Vinjam - 9820719457 - vishnu.vinjam@gmail.com,100050066@iitb.ac.in


Aim and Motivation :

Samwad is a student group set-up to start a dialogue, a conversation with the society. It aims to connect students to the outside society and realities of life to understand various socio-technical issues and its manifestations and work towards finding sustainable solutions. Samwad started out as a small interest group and has now turned into one of the most active clubs on campus.These funds will be used to broaden the scope of activities and take up more challenging initiatives and projects.

University Rover Challenge

Project Leader: Varun S S - 9920136821 - varunss1993@gmail.com

  • Manvi Dhawan - 9619417531 - manvidhawan1993@gmail.com
  • Nidhi Desai - 9819239255 - nidhi1374desai@gmail.com


Aim and Motivation :

Aim:The University Rover Challenge project will act as a base for developing an actual space grade version of the rover which would one day be a part of Indian Mars Mission. The aim is to build a rover which adheres to the the problem statement of the URC -2014 competition and promote an interest in space technologies among the students in the process.

Motivation:Encourage more students into space research by acting as a platform where students can have hands-on experience on space technologies.


Funds Sanctioned: Rs. 50,000


Phase 1 Details :

Deliverables: The robotic arm and communication system of the rover will be completed. The arm will be capable of dexterous tasks like pushing buttons, gripping etc. The communication system allows live video feed and rover control for over 1 km range. These constitute an integral part of the rover systems
