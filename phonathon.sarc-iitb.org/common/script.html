HATS
Let me tell you about HATS – Hostel Alumni Team Stewardship
Initiated in 2006 in H7, this initiative is basically an attempt to reconnect alumni to their hostels & help create funds for the welfare of hostel may it be for the infrastructure or mess workers' good. As of today the figures tell around 269 donors have gathered 1.15 crore rupees. & from this cause we have a state of the art gym in H4, purchases of musical instruments, Home Theatre for H2, book shelves for H7 & many such stories. This cause has also supported 200 mess workers from the Mess Workers Incentive Funds.

We also have another such initiative by the name of Young Faculty Award  - YFA

YFA
It is to attract the best faculty members  at IIT Bombay. YFA awards a grant of 1lakh annually uptill 3 years to newly recruited faculty members. Around 26 new hires were made last year & a total of 75 hires could avail this opportunity from current funds ranging at around 6.5 crores net.
