        SAM, this year will have many events spread over both days.

Break The Ice : This is a mentor mentee lunch as part of the ASMP program to get over the initial inhibitions between an alumnus mentor and his student mentee.The lunch is also open to all the alumni invited to/ attending SAM.

Mock Interviews : We will have an alumnus hold a fundae session on how to prepare for and give interviews when sitting for placements. This will be followed by a few interviews that’ll be taken immediately after the session by the speaker. After which we will have various alumni taking interviews in the various hostels to students who have registered for it before.

Beyond The Horizons : We invite alumni who dared to beyond the horizons. Those who dared to take the road not taken and pursued their passion. The alumni share their success stories, their inspiration and experiences along the way.

Panel Discussion : We invite a panel of alumni, a professor and a student representative to discuss and debate over an issue or a topic that plagues every IITian.

Alumni Sports Meet : The Alumni Students sports meet has been held the last 2 years and this year we are holding it together with SAM. Last year a total of 9 sports were played and we had over 70 registrations.

Technical Display :  Let’s not forget the “T” part in IIT. The students at IIT Bombay are as enthusiastic as ever when it comes to tech activities. We will have a display of the various projects by our students, both theoretical and practical.

Alumni Cultural Weekend : Spread over 2 days, we will have various cultural competitions open to the alumni and we’ll end SAM on a high note with performances from both the students and alumni.
