What is Speed Mentoring ?

For the last three years the Alumni Student Mentorship Program has been trying to help students of IIT Bombay by providing them with experienced alumni as mentors.Over these 3 years it has come to our notice that many students wish to gain some kind of exposure/knowledge of the various career fields they could get into. In our view no one but our alumni, who have been there and done that, could give a better insight into the finer details of every sector. Keeping this in mind, the Alumni Student Mentorship Program in collaboration with the Career Cell of IIT Bombay is proud to announce the launch of a new initiative - Speed Mentoring - from January 2014. The main focus of this initiative would be to help students have short one-to-one interactions with alumni who have excelled in different fields thus helping them get a better idea about the field.

Rules and Policies

1. The allotment for the “Speed Mentoring” Initiative is done on the discretion of the ASMP team and Career Cell, keeping in mind various factors like the program in which the student is registered, preferences of time slots, statement of purpose of the student. Once done the allotment will be final and the ASMP/Career Cell Team will not entertain requests to change mentors.

2. Mentors will be given basic details of the students who have been allotted to him/her. Hence students are requested to kindly fill all the details with care. Any improper details undermining the credibility of the programme will result in immediate disqualification.

3. There will be a confirmed list of interested students and a wait list. The confirmed students would be called 1 hour before their slot to confirm whether they would be coming or not. If they are not able to make it, then some student from the wait list would be invited instead.

4. No personal requests/favors will be entertained regarding allotment of any particular mentor to any particular mentee.

6. No contact details of the mentors except skype-id would be disclosed to the mentee.

7. Speed mentoring provides an opportunity for a one time interaction of 30 min. If the mentee wishes to seek long term guidance from mentors, he/she is requested to register for Spring/Autumn phases of Alumni Student Mentorship Programme

Frequently Asked Questions

What is the aim of the program?

Through this program we aim to give students a better picture as to what they can expect in the many different career opportunities that are available to them. We hope to achieve this by setting up short one-to-one interactions with alumni from various fields.

How is the allotment process carried out ?

After the students fill in the registration form allotment is done keeping in mind factors such as- the year in which the student is studying as well as his interests/SOP.

How will the interactions take place ?

The interactions will be through the medium of Skype conference calls. The duration of these calls will typically be around 20-30 minutes and the student is expected to adhere to this time constraint.

Can mentees apply for multiple fields of interests during registration?

As the aim of the initiative is to give students as much exposure as possible they are invited to fill in multiple fields of interest during their registration.
