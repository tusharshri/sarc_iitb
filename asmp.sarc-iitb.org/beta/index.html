ASMP IIT Bombay

Mentoring is a brain to pick, an ear to listen, and a push in the right direction. With the aim of mending the broken link between students and alumni, Student Alumni Relations Cell launched Alumni Student Mentorship Program (ASMP).

Learn about what we do

Our Program emphasizes on:

1. Providing students a platform for one to one interaction with their esteemed alumni. 2. Rendering students with career guidance. Tailoring them for their future endeavors. 3. Catering to the betterment of alumni-student relationships by engaging them in fruitful conversations.
