First Look

Mentoring is a process for the informal transmission of knowledge, social capital, and the psychosocial support perceived by the recipient as relevant to work, career, or professional development; mentoring entails informal communication, usually face-to-face and during a sustained period of time, between a person who is perceived to have greater relevant knowledge, wisdom, or experience (the mentor)

This program initiated by the Student Alumni Relations Cell (SARC), IITB aims at improving the Alumni network within the institute and brings closer the students and the esteemed and experienced alumni.The students at every point stand at the threshold of limitless possibilities, but a mentor's insight would help them streamline their priorities and goals and thus bridge the gap between the outside and inside world. Alumni with their modest investment of time will not only develop long lasting relations, have more intimate connection with their college but they will also have the satisfa ction of having contributed to the comfort and education of future generation of IITians.Mentor@Intern an initiative by SARC IIT Bombay and IIT Bombay AA on the similar lines of ASMP To know more about it click here
